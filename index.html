WhatsApp notifications kaise kaam karte hain?
+
Jab supplier kisi order ko accept, deny, ya deliver mark karta hai — SupplySarthi automatically client ke phone pe WhatsApp message bhejta hai. Tumhe manually kuch type nahi karna. Messages pre-defined professional templates pe based hote hain. Client hamesha informed rehta hai — tumhara koi effort nahi.
Daily aur Monthly invoicing mein kya difference hai?
+
Daily invoicing mein ek din ke saare delivered orders ka ek invoice ban jaata hai — jo businesses roz settle karte hain unke liye. Monthly invoicing mein ek poore mahine ke saare delivered orders ek invoice mein compile hote hain — month-end billing ke liye. Dono options mein GST automatic calculate hota hai.
Site-wise pricing kaise set karte hain?
+
Client management mein jaake ek client ki multiple sites add kar sakte ho. Har site ke liye alag-alag price list set karo — item-wise. Jab client usi site se order place karta hai, toh system automatically correct rate apply kar deta hai. Koi manual calculation nahi.
Kya mujhe Google Workspace ka paid plan lena padega?
+
Nahi. SupplySarthi free Google account pe bhi chalta hai — Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Drive. Google Workspace paid plan ki zaroorat nahi. Haan, agar tumhara business already workspace pe hai, toh wahan bhi perfectly kaam karega.

+
Zyada se zyada 1 ghanta. Business settings configure karo, clients add karo, items add karo, aur web app deploy karo. Har step simple hai. Aur setup support hum dete hain — tum akele nahi ho.
Kya mobile pe kaam karta hai?
+
Haan, SupplySarthi fully mobile-responsive hai. Laptop, desktop, tablet, phone — kisi bhi browser mein kholo aur use karo. Koi app download karne ki zaroorat nahi.
Agar koi problem aaye toh support milega?
