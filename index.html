WhatsApp se aane wale orders ko ek central system mein track karo — Pending se Delivered tak, har step visible aur controlled.

  • Real-time order status tracking — Pending → Confirmed → Dispatched → Delivered
  • Delivered vs Ordered quantity alag-alag track hoti hai
  • Ek din ka Demand Summary — warehouse planning ke liye
  • Print-ready Delivery Challan auto-generate hota hai
  • Client-wise aur site-wise order history with filters
  • One-click reorder — client ki last order repeat karo instantly

FAQ

Sawaal jo sabke mann mein hote hain 🤔

Kya sach mein koi subscription nahi hai?
+
Bilkul nahi. SupplySarthi ek one-time ₹35,000 ka setup hai. Ek baar pay karo, system lifetime tumhara. Koi monthly fee nahi, koi yearly renewal nahi, koi "plan expire" nahi. Data tumhara Google Drive mein hai — koi vendor lock-in nahi.
3 months ke baad kya hoga? Extra charges?
+
3 months ke andar koi bhi bug fix, sheet crash, ya issue — bilkul free. 3 months ke baad, agar koi technical issue aaye ya naya feature chahiye ho, toh ₹5,000 per request charge lagega. Yeh sirf tab pay karna hai jab tumhe actually zaroorat ho — koi forced subscription nahi, koi auto-renewal nahi. System waise bhi kaam karta rahega — bas support on-demand basis pe hoga.
Kya mujhe Google Workspace ka paid plan lena padega?
+
Nahi. SupplySarthi free Google account pe bhi chalta hai — Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Drive. Google Workspace paid plan ki zaroorat nahi. Haan, agar tumhara business already workspace pe hai, toh wahan bhi perfectly kaam karega.
Kitne clients aur orders handle ho sakte hain?
+
Google Sheets mein practically unlimited rows hoti hain. 50 clients ho ya 500, 100 orders roz ho ya 1000 — system same tarah kaam karega. Koi per-client ya per-order limit nahi hai SupplySarthi mein.
Kya yeh GST compliant hai?
+
Haan. SupplySarthi IGST aur CGST+SGST dono support karta hai — state ke hisaab se automatic. HSN codes, GST percentage per item, GSTIN on invoices, reverse charge — sab supported hai. Invoice format professional aur print-ready hai.
Setup karne mein kitna time lagega?
+
Zyada se zyada 1 ghanta. Business settings configure karo, clients add karo, items add karo, aur web app deploy karo. Har step simple hai. Aur setup support hum dete hain — tum akele nahi ho.
Kya mobile pe kaam karta hai?
+
Haan, SupplySarthi fully mobile-responsive hai. Laptop, desktop, tablet, phone — kisi bhi browser mein kholо aur use karo. Koi app download karne ki zaroorat nahi.
